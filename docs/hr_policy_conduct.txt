SYNTHETIX AI — CODE OF CONDUCT
Version 3.2 | Approved by Board: March 2024

Employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional conduct.
Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind are strictly prohibited.
All conflicts of interest must be disclosed to the Ethics Committee within 14 days of identification.
Employees must not engage with competitors on matters related to proprietary technology.
Breach of this code may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
Report concerns anonymously via the Ethics Hotline: 1800-SYNTH-ETH
