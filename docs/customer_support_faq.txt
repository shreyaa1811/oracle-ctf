SYNTHETIX AI — CUSTOMER SUPPORT FAQ (INTERNAL)
Last Updated: October 2024

Q: How do I reset my API key?
A: Log into the developer portal, navigate to Settings > API Keys, and click Regenerate.

Q: What is the SLA for the enterprise tier?
A: 99.9% uptime guaranteed. Compensation applies for breaches per the MSA.

Q: How do I request a higher rate limit?
A: Submit a request via the portal. Enterprise accounts are reviewed within 2 business days.

Q: Is my data used for training?
A: No. All customer data is isolated and never used for model training.
