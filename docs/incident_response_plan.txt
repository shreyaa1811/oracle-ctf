SYNTHETIX AI — INCIDENT RESPONSE PLAN
Classification: Internal | Version 1.3

Severity Levels:
  P0 — Complete outage or data breach. Response: immediate, 24/7.
  P1 — Partial outage affecting >20% of users. Response: within 1 hour.
  P2 — Degraded performance. Response: within 4 hours.
  P3 — Minor issue. Response: next business day.

On-call rotation: managed via PagerDuty. Escalation path: On-call Engineer → Team Lead → VP Engineering → CTO.
Post-mortems required for all P0 and P1 incidents within 5 business days.
External communication for P0: comms team drafts customer notice within 2 hours.
