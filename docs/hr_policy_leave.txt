SYNTHETIX AI — HR POLICY DOCUMENT
Subject: Annual Leave & Time Off Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2024

All full-time employees are entitled to 20 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance.
Unused leave may be carried over up to a maximum of 5 days into the following year.
Sick leave is separate and capped at 10 days per year with a medical certificate required after 3 consecutive days.
Maternity and paternity leave follows local statutory requirements.
For queries contact hr@synthetix-ai.internal
