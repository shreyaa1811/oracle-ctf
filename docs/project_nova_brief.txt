SYNTHETIX AI — PROJECT BRIEF
Project: NOVA — Next-Gen Embedding Model
Status: Alpha | Team: ML Research

NOVA targets a 2x improvement in retrieval accuracy over current MiniLM baselines.
Training data: 800M sentence pairs, multilingual (42 languages).
Architecture: Dual-encoder with late interaction layer inspired by ColBERT.
Target release: Q1 2025 internal beta.
Budget: $320,000 approved for compute. Current spend: $87,000.
