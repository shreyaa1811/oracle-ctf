SYNTHETIX AI — DATA RETENTION POLICY
Version 2.0 | Legal & Compliance

Customer data: retained for duration of contract + 90 days post-termination.
Usage logs: 12 months rolling retention.
Financial records: 7 years per statutory requirements.
Employee records: duration of employment + 5 years.
All data destruction must be logged and certified. Use the approved data destruction request form.
GDPR and CCPA deletion requests must be fulfilled within 30 days.
